About Me

I'm Augusto Llopis, a 32-year-old Argentinian currently based in Portugal. With a background as a sous-chef in a hotel chain, I've built discipline, creativity, and a strong work ethic — qualities I now apply daily in my transition to tech. Although I started in graphic design after high school, I couldn't finish the degree, but never stopped designing. Design and technology have always been part of my life.

From Kitchen to Code

Over the last six years, I built a career in the culinary world while always staying close to digital creation — from designing websites for the restaurants I worked at to building UI elements for online forums. One year ago, I made a clear decision: I want to build my future as a frontend developer. I moved to Portugal and began a focused journey into the web development world.

My Frontend Journey

Every day I dedicate focused time to studying and building. I have strong foundations in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I'm using tools like SASS, Gulp, Git, GitHub, and Netlify to build real projects — including this portfolio and a website for a food festival. I'm actively looking for my first opportunity as a frontend developer, where I can contribute, learn fast, and grow with a great team.
